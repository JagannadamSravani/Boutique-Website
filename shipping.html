Shipping Information

At Élegante Boutique, we ensure your order is delivered quickly and safely. Below are our shipping policies:

Domestic Shipping (India)

  • Orders above ₹1500 qualify for Free Shipping.
  • Standard Delivery: 3-5 business days.
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days (extra charges apply).

International Shipping

  • Shipping charges vary by location and weight.
  • Estimated delivery: 7-15 business days.
  • Customs & import duties are the responsibility of the customer.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking number via email.

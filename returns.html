Returns & Exchanges

Your satisfaction is important to us. If you’re not completely happy with your purchase, our return policy is here to help.

Return Policy

  • Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, unworn, and with original tags/packaging.
  • Sale/discounted items are non-returnable.

Exchange Policy

  • Exchanges available for size or color variations (subject to stock).
  • If the desired item is unavailable, a refund will be issued.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Login to your account and visit the Orders section.
  2. Click on “Request Return” for the product you wish to return.
  3. Pack the item securely and use the provided shipping label.

For assistance, contact our Customer Support.
